Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a mock interview?

To start a mock interview, navigate to the "Interview Section" from the navigation menu, fill out your details, select the type of interview, and click "Start Mock Interview."

What types of interviews are available?

We offer several types of interviews, including technical, HR, behavioral, and case study interviews. You can choose the type based on your needs during registration.

Is the AI interview tailored to my experience level?

Yes, our AI adapts the questions and scenarios based on your selected experience level: Fresher, Mid-Level, or Senior.

How do I get feedback on my mock interview?

After completing the mock interview, you'll receive detailed feedback with areas for improvement, tips, and a score breakdown.
